photographer anthony barbour , 33 , from liverpool , turns parks , village and beaches into their own little worlds . he spends hours slowly rotating in one spot taking the 50 pictures he needs to create just one planet uses a canon 1100 d dslr with either a 18-55 mm kit lens or a tokina 11-16 mm to shoot the photographs . then uses photoshop to stitch and layer the photos together to create incredible and mind bending shots .
