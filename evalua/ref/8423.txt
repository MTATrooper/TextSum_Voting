alanna and stephen goetzinger lost their daughter rana when she was stillborn during a waterbirth at their home in 2012 . rana was found to have died of meconium aspiration , a condition where a foetus inhales their faeces before birth , preventing them from breathing . a coroner's report found that their midwife failed to recognise signs of the meconium , changed the time of birth , and whether there was signs of life .
