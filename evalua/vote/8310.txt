kenyan special forces took at least seven hours to respond to the brutal massacre at a university where nearly 150 people were killed by islamist militants , according to reports . elite troops were called in from nairobi to garissa , some 225 miles from the capital , to aid in the pre dawn attack on the university , but soldiers did not arrive until the afternoon , local media claims . the critique comes as one of the four gunmen responsible for the brutal mass murders has been identified as a lawyer son of a kenyan government official . earlier today , the interior ministry revealed the identity of one of the four gunmen responsible for the massacre on thursday .
