jail terms luigi arcuri left , 73 , nikolic giuliano centre , 37 and antonino ballistreri right , 45 , were each sentenced to two years and eight months for conspiracy to defraud and possession of counterfeit currency . using a flat pack wooden cabinet as a trojan horse , one of the thieves was to hide inside . the gang then planned to put real money in a briefcase and show it to a jeweller at a pre arranged meeting , before placing it on the floor . then , while a price was being agreed at a nearby table , the man in the cabinet would switch the case for one containing counterfeit notes . the gang posing as wealthy italians would then walk off with their money and the stolen jewellery . it was a cunning plot to steal more than 1.5 million in gold bullion and jewellery .
