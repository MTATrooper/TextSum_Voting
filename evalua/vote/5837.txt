a man has been charged after he allegedly held a woman hostage at knifepoint for more than seven hours in a central melbourne restaurant . victoria police arrested 35 year old abijath desikan following the 7.5 hour siege at riverside quay . mr desikan was charged with one count of armed robbery , false imprisonment and assault with a weapon . the man , of no fixed address , faced an out of sessions hearing and was remanded in custody to appear in melbourne magistrates court tomorrow morning .
