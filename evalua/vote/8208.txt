for more than 2,000 years , invaders have been fighting over glorious kefalonia . romans , the kingdom of naples , the venetian republic , the ottoman empire , the french , british and , during the second world war , the italians and germans have all left their mark . from lourdas bay you can see zakynthos island , where the beautiful shipwreck beach pictured is found . the famous curve of myrtos beach , as well as many little inland villages , are seen on the road to fiscardo . richard johnson rejoices in unspoiled kefalonia .
