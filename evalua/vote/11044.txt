one us navy seal died and another was critically injured on friday while training in a pool at a virginia military base , a navy spokesman said . the two members of the navy's elite sea , air , land teams were found at the bottom of the pool by another service member around 3 pm local time at joint expeditionary base little creek fort story . the names of the seals were withheld pending notification of next of kin . they were swimming . it was not dive training .
